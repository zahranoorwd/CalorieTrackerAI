Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CalorieTrackerAI ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Food log entries (food names, calorie counts, macronutrient data, serving sizes, timestamps)
  • Daily water intake records
  • Nutritional goals and preferences (calorie targets, macro targets)
  • Theme and display preferences
  • Images you choose to scan for nutritional analysis

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • App usage patterns (screens visited, features used)
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address, not precise GPS)

1.3 Third-Party Services

When you use the food scanning feature, images you submit are sent to OpenAI's API for nutritional analysis. OpenAI processes these images in accordance with their own privacy policy. We do not store your images on our servers — they are transmitted directly to OpenAI and discarded after analysis.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (calorie tracking, food logging, nutritional analysis)
  • Store your food logs, goals, and preferences locally on your device
  • Analyze food images via third-party AI services to provide nutritional estimates
  • Improve the App's performance, features, and user experience
  • Generate aggregated, anonymized analytics to understand usage patterns
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

Your food logs, goals, and preferences are stored locally on your device using secure on-device storage. We do not maintain a centralized database of your personal nutrition data.

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • With OpenAI for food image analysis (images only, no personal identifiers)
  • With service providers who assist in app analytics and crash reporting
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or the public

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Portability: All your data is stored locally on your device. You can view it at any time within the App.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual food entries, clear your history, or uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • Camera Permissions: You can revoke camera and photo library access at any time through your device settings. This will disable the food scanning feature but the rest of the App will continue to function.
  • Opt-Out: You may stop using the App at any time. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

7. International Data Transfers

If you use the food scanning feature, images may be processed by OpenAI's servers, which may be located in the United States or other countries. By using this feature, you consent to the transfer of this data to these locations.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
